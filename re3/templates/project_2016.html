The LakeShore

The LakeShore, the most anticipated Downtown Toronto condo by Concord Adex, is located at Lake Shore Blvd. and Bathurst St. in the Concord CityPlace neighborhood. Rising majestically above an iconic structure, The LakeShore condo will be one-of-a-kind. Exceptional lake and city views. A 50,000 sq. ft. Loblaws flagship supermarket, 11,000 sq. ft. restaurant, and 88,000 sq. ft. of additional retail. Refined suite design. Inspired features and finishes and a compendium of life, health, and fitness amenities like none other.
